How it works

1

Download & unzip

Download the zip for your platform and unzip it. Windows: Then double-click "Install SIM Scraper" to set up a Desktop shortcut.

2

Run the scraper

Mac: Double-click "Run Scraper" in the unzipped folder. Windows: Double-click "SIM Price Scraper" on your Desktop. First run auto-installs Python if needed.

3

Upload the results

The scraper saves a file to your Desktop. Come back here, click "Upload Data" in the dashboard, and select the file.
