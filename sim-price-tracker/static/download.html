How it works

1

Download & install

Mac: Unzip and drag "SIM Price Scraper" to your Applications folder. Windows: Unzip and double-click "Install SIM Scraper".

2

Run the scraper

Mac: Open "SIM Price Scraper" from Applications. Windows: Double-click "SIM Price Scraper" on your Desktop. First run installs Python if needed.

3

Upload the results

The scraper saves a file to your Desktop. Come back here, click "Upload Data" in the dashboard, and select the file.
